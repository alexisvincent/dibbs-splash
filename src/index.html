Second hand textbooks

New University textbooks are really expensive... and finding people to buy books from is a shlep.

Dibbs matches you with other students who are looking to buy or sell textbooks. So you can spend more on the important things in life, at Bohemia.

Accommodation

The hunt for a new apartment can often be a stressful endeavour for Maties.

We feel your pain.

Dibbs connects you with other students looking for flatmates or moving out of diggs, as well as Agents offering housing. You’re welcome.

Lift Sharing

Some mates are having a braai in Cape Town. But you don’t have transport. Who you gna call? Ghost Bus.. no.

Dibbs puts you in touch with other students already driving to the area. So that you can share the petrol cost and maybe make a new friend.

Second Hand

Lets be real, most of us are pretty broke most of the time. Who wants to buy a new fridge, when Flippie down the road has a cooler box he’s not using any more.

Dibbs has a dedicated second hand page, so that Flippie’s cooler box can be beautifully presented, and he doesn’t have to keep commenting on his own post to up-vote it.

Campus Wide Games

Dibbs will be hosting campus wide games all year through. These will be supported by our mobile app, and we’re going to have a lot of fun doing things only possible with the support of technology.

We’re kicking off with a QR Code treasure hunt on campus in the first week of Feb, so be sure to look out for the posters, there are some awesome prizes up for grabs (including an iPad mini).

And so, so much more.

Dibbs is expanding features rapidly, and you can expect many cool additions this year. In fact, we’d really like to hear what you’d like us to build. We are aiming to create a simple channel for feedback on the site so that we can make Dibbs the most kick ass society on campus.

You’ll be able to speak directly to the Dibbs team through the messaging module on the app. So be sure to let us know what you think.
